Currently I am writing a book called Bless Your Heart, which features a photographer named Zoe as the main protagonist. Zoe grew up in the rural south of the US where her grandfather often took her hunting.

She mostly shoots weddings and events with a digital camera for a living. However, she is obsessed with analogue photography, and she will bring her cameras when she goes hunting with her grandfather.

Most of these images document my own experiences that have influenced this book, while the others attempt to capture the horrors Zoe experiences later on in the book.

Zoe would enjoy shooting with the cameras I used in this series. I documented a few hunts with my uncle using a 35mm Canon, which was not staged. The images of the woman (a dear cousin of mine) are staged, and I captured her stunning portraits with a medium format Hassleblad.
