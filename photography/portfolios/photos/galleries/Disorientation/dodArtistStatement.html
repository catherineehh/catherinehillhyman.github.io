In my experience with seasonal affective disorder, my mind perceives reality differently with respect to changes in intensity and duration of sunlight throughout the year. Color, perspective, and sound become a haze of warped space and time.

These images portray the sense of confusion and heavy emotions that I personally feel in an attempt to understand my own mind during such times when this psychological disease invades my subconscious. Exploring the darkness that comes when the days shrink has allowed me to appreciate the light, and I also hope to celebrate it with this project so that I may find a way to overcome the darkness when it returns.
